Our History

1942 Humble Beginnings

Thomas David Pulliam was born October 2nd, 1942, the eldest of 4 children, to Thomas David Pulliam Sr. and Lillian Lamb in Sandersville Georgia.

After being honorably discharged from the Army, David Pulliam went on to become the assitant manager at Winn-Dixie grocery in Augusta Georgia.

It was during this time that David would take his first steps into the plumbing industry.

1971-1979 Pulliam Supply Company

Being the hardworking and ambitious man he was, David Pulliam began landscaping for his local plumbing supply store in Augusta on the weekends.

This turned into a job opportunity for Mr. Pulliam and before long, he had worked his way up to credit manager within the company.

Over the course of the next 8 years, Mr. Pulliam honed his skills, learning everything he could about the industry, and would eventually break off on his own.

In 1979, Pulliam Supply Company was born.
